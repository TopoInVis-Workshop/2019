Registration

Registration fee and payment procedure

The registration fee for the workshop and what it covers is described below:

Event Fee Details
TopoInVis 2019 (June 17-19)
  • SEK 7000 (Early registration, by May 30, 2019)
  • SEK 7500 (Late registration)
  • Accommodation at the conference venue (Blommenhof Hotel, Nyköping) during the night of June 16.
  • All meals and accommodation on June 17 and 18.
  • Breakfast, lunch and tea on June 19.
TTK Hackathon (June 16) SEK 1500
  • Dinner and accommodation at the conference venue (Blommenhof Hotel, Nyköping) for the night of June 15.
  • All meals on June 16.

Please note that you do not have to book a separate hotel room for your stay or go out for meals outside the conference venue, everything would be provided for during the conference at the venue itself.

Payment procedure

The registration fee can only be paid via wire transfer. Please transfer the total fee amount within a week of registering for the workshop and/or hackathon. We will send you an acknowledgement after the receipt of the registration fee. Note that the registration will be complete only after the fee is successfully received by us.
