TopoInVis 2019

2019, Nyköping, Sweden

Accommodation

The workshop will take place during at the Blommenhof Hotel in Nyköping, Sweden. The accommodation for all participants will be available there. Registered participants can check in their rooms on the evening of June 16 if they are participating only in the TopoInVis, and on the evening of June 15 if they are also participating in TTK Hackathon. The meals will also be provided at the conference venue during their stay.
